Sisig

Description

Pork sisig is a popular Filipino dish. It is composed of minced pork, chopped onion, and chicken liver. It is a favorite dish for pulutan.

Ingredients

  • Pork Cheek
  • Bay Leaf
  • Black Peppercorn
  • MAGGI® Magic Sarap® 8g
  • Chicken Liver Pate
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Calf Brain
  • Garlic
  • Red Onion
  • Hot Chili Peppers
  • Calamansi Juice

Steps

  1. Simmer pork in water with bay leaves and peppercorn until tender. Drain and set aside to cool.
    Cut into thick slices and season with 1 sachet of MAGGI MAGIC SARAP®. Coat with 2 tbsp of oil. Grill until golden brown. Coarsely chop and set aside.
  2. Season chicken liver with ½ sachet of MAGGI MAGIC SARAP®. Coat with 1 tbsp of oil and grill until golden brown. Coarsely chop and add to pork.
  3. Simmer calf brain in water for 10 minutes (optional). Strain, coarsely chop and add to pork and liver.
  4. Add garlic, onion and chili. Season with remaining ½ sachet of MAGGI MAGIC SARAP®, MAGGI Savor Classic, calamansi juice,
    sugar and pepper. Gently mix. Preheat a clean sizzling plate, add 1 cup of Sisig, mix and serve immediately.
